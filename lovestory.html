To Hanyu

This is the first birthday I spend with you. Before meeting you, I can't imagine there will be such nice person appearing in my life. There are many excellent people in the world, but to me, you are the unique one. Just like what I wrote to you before, you remind me of that heavy snow in my childhood, and will leave in my mind forever.

There will always be quarrels and barriers in the future. But I know whatever happens, as long as we are making progress together, as long as Hanyu doesn't give up, I will always be with you. Thank you for tolerating me for my little temper and shortcomings. Also, thank you again for appearing in my life, at the best age and the best time.
